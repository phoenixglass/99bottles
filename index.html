Why Ninety 9 Bottles?

Your Local Bottle Shop, Three Locations Strong

Ninety 9 Bottles is a locally owned and operated bottle shop serving Fairfield County since the beginning. We pride ourselves on a handpicked selection, fair prices, and a team that genuinely loves what they do.

🍺

Craft Beer Haven

An outstanding rotating selection of local, regional, and hard-to-find craft beers that keeps enthusiasts coming back.

🍷

Wine for Every Palate

From everyday bottles to special-occasion finds, our wine section covers every style, region, and price point.

🥃

Premium Spirits

Whiskey, bourbon, gin, rum, tequila, and more—curated with care and priced competitively.

🎉

Tastings & Events

We regularly host in-store tastings so you can discover your next favorite before you buy.

🤝

Expert Staff

Our passionate team is always ready to help you find the perfect bottle for any occasion or budget.

📦

Special Orders

Can't find what you're looking for? We'll special order it. Delivery and curbside pickup available too.
